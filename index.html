Welcome to our ABCBio Science Academy

Today's world is governed by the advancement of science. Science dictates the path we take to engage, investigate, act and reflect. In this rapidly changing era of exponential technologies, unfortunately, our country still lies in a vicious cycle of conventional curriculum and outworn teaching method.

ABCBioScience is an educational platform that will allow students to explore the world by the means of science. Providing a unique way to learn and to apply scientific wisdom to engage with the diversity of the Earth system and beyond, ABCBioScience aims at making students knowledgeable about How Things Really Work! We are inclined to help students connect the dots of the biological and physical world, resulting in a fully-fledged understanding of the inner Beauty of Science!
